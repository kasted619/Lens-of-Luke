About

Meet Luke Pugh

I’m a photographer based in San Diego, California, though my work often takes me around the world. I specialize in using the Canon 5D, known as “The Classic,” and have spent years mastering this legendary camera. Over time, I’ve built a collection of these cameras to ensure I always have top-quality gear for my work, which spans from small newspapers to large corporate projects. If you’re interested in owning a Canon 5D Classic, I offer a sourcing service and have a selection of premium bodies available—please inquire below.

My passion for photography began in the late ’90s during the final years of the film era. I spent countless hours in my high school darkroom, fascinated by film’s unique colors and texture, despite the high cost—about $1.25 per shot. While in college, I discovered digital photography when a professor introduced a groundbreaking camera: the Canon 5D. Seeing instant images displayed and printed was like witnessing the future. Yet, the 5D’s digital images still held the rich, film-like quality I loved. Though it came with a hefty price tag, I eventually purchased my own Canon 5D, and it became the foundation of my photography career—capturing countless moments across the globe.
